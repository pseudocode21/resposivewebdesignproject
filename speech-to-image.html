Frequently Asked Questions

How does the Speech-to-Image feature work?

This feature uses speech recognition to convert your voice into text, which is then analyzed by an AI model to generate a relevant image based on your spoken description.

Which browsers support speech recognition?

Speech recognition works best in Chrome, Edge, and Safari. Firefox and some mobile browsers might have limited support.

How can I speak for better results?

Speak clearly and include specific details such as colors, objects, mood, and style. The more descriptive your voice prompt, the better the generated image.

Is my voice data saved or shared?

No, your voice data is processed locally in the browser and is not stored or shared. Only the transcribed text is used to generate the image.

Can I download and use the images?

Yes, all generated images can be downloaded and are free to use for personal and non-commercial purposes. Please review licensing if used commercially.

Why isn't my microphone working?

Make sure you've allowed microphone access in your browser settings. Try refreshing the page or using a different browser if the issue persists.
