Frequently Asked Questions

How does the Image-to-Text feature work?

This feature analyzes the uploaded image using AI and machine learning to extract either a descriptive caption (captioning) or readable text content (OCR) from the image.

What types of images work best?

High-quality images with clear content yield the best results. For text extraction, images with good lighting and readable fonts are ideal.

Is my uploaded image stored or shared?

No, all image processing is handled securely. Uploaded images are not stored or shared and are discarded after generating the text.

Can it read handwritten text?

Yes, but accuracy may vary depending on the clarity and neatness of the handwriting. Printed text gives better results than cursive or messy writing.

Can I copy or download the extracted text?

Yes, once the text is extracted, you can easily copy or download it for your use.

Is there a file size limit for image uploads?

Yes, the maximum upload size is typically 5MB per image. Try compressing or resizing large files before uploading.
